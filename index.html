About Me

Hi! I'm a PhD candidate at the Institut de Robòtica i Informàtica Industrial (CSIC-UPC) in Barcelona. My work focuses on making assistive robots more adaptive and transparent when interacting with multiple users. I'm particularly interested in how we can use computational argumentation to resolve preference conflicts—for example, when a caregiver and a patient have different goals—and how robots can explain their decisions so that users can contest and influence them over time.


Research interests: Human-Robot Interaction, Assistive Robotics, Gradual Argumentation, Multi-User Personalisation, and Explainable AI.
