About Me

I am a Research Scientist specialized in Human-AI Interaction, Explainable AI, and computational social science. My work bridges theoretical machine learning with real-world adoption, focusing on transparent and fair AI systems. I lead interdisciplinary projects at the intersection of cognition and deep learning. Passionate about open science and mentoring early-career researchers. Currently exploring neuro-symbolic methods and interactive interpretability.


Research interests: Interpretable ML, Human-AI collaboration, Multimodal Learning, AI Ethics, and Digital Humanities.
